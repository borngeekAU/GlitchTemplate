OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document outlines the content flow and structure for the GLITCH_STITCH
creative project. All content follows the "recovered data" narrative device,
presenting original creative work as analog transmissions recovered from
corrupted data streams.


CONTENT PILLARS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. TRANSMISSION SERIES (Primary Content)
   - Short-form video content (15-60 seconds)
   - Platforms: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
   - Frequency: 3-5 posts per week
   - Theme: "Recovered" footage with glitch aesthetic

2. SIGNAL NOISE (Audio Content)
   - Atmospheric audio elements
   - Background tracks for videos
   - Standalone audio posts (where supported)

3. STATIC FRAMES (Visual Content)
   - Still images with glitch effects
   - "Pause-worthy" frames hidden in videos
   - Promotional graphics

4. DATA LOGS (Text Content)
   - Captions and descriptions
   - Narrative text elements
   - Community engagement posts


CONTENT CALENDAR TEMPLATE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

WEEK STRUCTURE:

Monday:     TRANSMISSION upload (primary video)
Tuesday:    Community engagement / replies
Wednesday:  STATIC FRAME post (image)
Thursday:   Behind-the-scenes or process content
Friday:     TRANSMISSION upload (primary video)
Saturday:   Engagement / trend participation
Sunday:     Planning and content creation


TRANSMISSION FORMAT GUIDE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STRUCTURE (15-60 second videos):

0:00-0:03   - Static/noise intro (brand recognition)
0:03-0:05   - "SIGNAL DETECTED" text overlay
0:05-0:XX   - Main content with glitch effects
0:XX-0:XX   - Hidden frame (pause-worthy element)
0:XX-END    - "TRANSMISSION ENDED" + static outro

REQUIRED ELEMENTS:
[ ] Opening static/noise
[ ] GLITCH_STITCH branding
[ ] Main content segment
[ ] At least one "hidden" frame
[ ] Closing static/noise
[ ] Appropriate audio treatment


HIDDEN FRAME STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PURPOSE:
- Encourage viewers to pause and explore
- Drive traffic to landing page
- Create community engagement (screenshot sharing)
- Build mystery around the narrative

PLACEMENT:
- 1-3 frames within each video
- Positioned at unexpected moments
- Contains: QR code, URL, cryptic message, or visual cue

TYPES:
1. QR Code Frame - Links to landing page
2. Text Frame - Cryptic message or coordinates
3. Image Frame - Unique graphic or symbol
4. URL Frame - Direct website link

ENGAGEMENT GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

RESPOND TO:
- Questions about the narrative
- Technical questions about effects
- Collaboration inquiries
- Positive engagement

MAINTAIN CHARACTER:
- Responses can be "in character" as GLITCH_STITCH
- Use technical/data terminology
- Keep mystery alive
- Be helpful while staying on-brand

- Acknowledge viewers who find hidden frames
- Create inside jokes and references
- Encourage user-generated content
- Feature community discoveries
