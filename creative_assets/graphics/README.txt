NAMING CONVENTION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Format: {CATEGORY}_{DESCRIPTION}_{SIZE/VARIANT}.{EXT}

Examples:
- logo_primary_1080x1080.png
- overlay_scanlines_1920x1080.png
- frame_qr_landing_page.png
- banner_youtube_2560x1440.png


RECOMMENDED SPECIFICATIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LOGOS:
- Format: PNG (transparent background)
- Resolution: Multiple sizes (256px, 512px, 1024px)
- Color modes: Full color + monochrome versions

OVERLAYS:
- Format: PNG (with transparency)
- Resolution: Match video resolution (1080x1920 or 1920x1080)
- Opacity: Pre-set or adjustable in editing

SOCIAL MEDIA SIZES:
- TikTok Profile: 200x200px
- Instagram Profile: 320x320px
- YouTube Profile: 800x800px
- YouTube Banner: 2560x1440px
- Instagram Story: 1080x1920px


BRAND COLORS (Reference):

BACKGROUNDS:
